About Me

Basic Information

I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin class of 2020 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Communication Studies, focusing on Corporate Communications. I also completed the Computer Science Certificate, and lastly I minored in Korean Language. I worked as an Event Planner/Marketing Team member for a small non-profit called B*TRU Arts. I founded and led a Community Service Ministry where, to put it simply, we went out every week to feed the homeless.

Now

If you’ve seen my GitHub profile, you’re probably wondering why there’s a huge gap for about a year. The main reason was I just took a year off after graduating. I’m not trying to create a sob story, others have it way worse than me, but my family owns a small business and money was tight because of COVID-19. I pretty much spent a year making some quick cash as a Tiff’s Treats delivery driver. I also wasn’t confident in my computer science skills enough to apply for a job during that time. Things are better now financially and I spent the recent weeks learning new technologies to make myself a more attractive candidate and to give myself more confidence as an applicant.

Goals

By the end of 2023, I want to become a Senior Developer but also have the mentality to act like a Great Senior Developer. I want to confidently make decisions and be familiar and open minded enough to lead and choose technologies for a team of developers. More importantly, I want to be a socially/emotionally intelligent Senior Developer that takes pride in not being arrogant, rude, or condescending to others. I don’t want to do that because of selfish intensions. I just understand how it feels to be the least intelligent person in the group, and I want to create an environment where people can feel comfortable and can freely make mistakes, so that they can effectively learn and become better developers.

Interests

I’m a nerd and like video games. Fan of basketball, watching definitely not playing. I do Calisthenics. I like music. Try to make some on the side for fun. Other than that I know my way around the other arts and their artsy softwares like Premiere, Animate, and Photoshop.
