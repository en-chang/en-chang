About Me

Basic Information

I graduated from The University of Texas at Austin class of 2020 with a Bachelor of Science degree in Communication Studies, focusing on Corporate Communications. I also completed the Computer Science Certificate, and lastly I minored in Korean Language. I worked as an Event Planner/Marketing Team member for a small non-profit called B*TRU Arts. I founded and led a Community Service Ministry where we, to put it simply, went out every week to feed the homeless.

What am I up to?

After graduating, I took a year off. I’m not trying to create a sob story, others had it way worse than me, but my family owns a small business and money was tight due to COVID-19. I pretty much spent a year making some quick cash as a Tiff’s Treats delivery driver to help out the family. I also wasn’t confident in my computer science skills to apply for a job during that time. Luckily things are better now financially, and I’ve spent the recent weeks learning new technologies to better myself.

Goals

By the end of 2023, I want to become a great Senior Developer in every aspect. I want to confidently make decisions and be familiar and open minded enough to lead and choose technologies for a team of developers. More importantly, I want to be a socially/emotionally intelligent Senior Developer that takes pride in not being arrogant, rude, or condescending to others. I don’t want to do that because of selfish intentions. I just understand how it feels to be the least intelligent person in the group, and I want to create an environment where people can feel comfortable and can freely make mistakes, so that they can effectively learn and become better developers.
